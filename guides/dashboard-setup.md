# Setting Up Your Student Dashboard

This guide will walk you through the initial configuration of your Student Dashboard. Following these steps ensures your profile is visible to instructors and your notifications are active.

## Before You Begin
* Ensure you have received your **Activation Code** via email.
* You must be using a modern browser (Chrome, Firefox, or Edge).

---

## Step 1: Activate Your Account
1. Navigate to the [Login Page](https://admin-portal-belinda.wuaze.com/login).
2. Click on the **"First Time User?"** link at the bottom of the form.
3. Enter your student email address and the 6-digit **Activation Code**.
4. Click **Verify**.

## Step 2: Configure Your Profile
Once verified, the system will prompt you to complete your profile:
1. **Upload a Photo:** Click the circular avatar icon. Select a professional headshot (JPG or PNG, max 2MB).
2. **Select Major:** Choose your current field of study from the dropdown menu.
3. **Bio:** Enter a short 2-line introduction.

## Step 3: Set Notification Preferences
To stay updated on grades and assignments:
1. Go to **Settings** > **Notifications**.
2. Toggle the **Email Notifications** switch to **ON**.
3. Click **Save Changes**.

## Troubleshooting
| Issue | Solution |
| :--- | :--- |
| **Code not received** | Check your Spam folder or click "Resend Code." |
| **Photo won't upload** | Ensure the file size is under 2MB and the format is correct. |
| **Login fails** | Clear your browser cache and try again. |
